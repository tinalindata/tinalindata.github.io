It might seem strange for an accountant to make such a radical decision to take up Data Science, but there is a certain logic behind this decision. Processing, storing, understanding, and using data is at the heart of the Data Science field, and as an accountant, this is something I understand. But unlike accounting, the possibilities of Data Science are much broader, not just in terms of personal financial success but also in terms of the things I could possibly accomplish in my lifetime.

As a previous accounting intern at Deloitte and Leo Burnett, I truly realized that a core part of every business is true understanding of the operation of the business and how to incorporate them into strategic business decisions.
